Youth Roller Hockey at Inline309, the perfect place for young hockey players to learn, develop their skills, and have fun!

We offer two age-specific leagues: a 12 and under league and an 18 and under league, both of which provide a safe and supportive environment for players to showcase their talents.

Our in-house league means that all games are played inside the Inline309 facility.

Our league emphasizes sportsmanship, teamwork, and skill-building, and is a great way for players to make new friends and become part of a community.

We believe in providing a high-quality hockey experience, which is why we offer experienced coaches who are dedicated to helping players develop their skills and reach their full potential.

At Inline309, we believe that hockey should be accessible to all young players, which is why we offer our league at an affordable price point.

So, whether your child is just starting out or has been playing for years, Youth Roller Hockey at Inline309 is the perfect place to learn, have fun, and take their game to the next level.
